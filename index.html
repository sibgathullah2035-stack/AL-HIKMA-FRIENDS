Biography

Mr. Musthaf Hudawi (U.P) is a dedicated educator with over 10 years of experience in Islamic education. He specializes in teaching Islamic Studies and Arabic Language to students at Darul Huda Islamic University.

Having served for seven years at the DHIU Maharashtra Campus, Mr. Hudawi has made remarkable contributions to the academic and spiritual development of his students. Alongside his teaching responsibilities, he also serves as the librarian of the campus, fostering a culture of reading, research, and intellectual curiosity among students.

With a deep understanding of both traditional Islamic knowledge and modern teaching methodologies, Mr. Hudawi has been instrumental in shaping the educational journey of countless students. His approach combines academic rigor with spiritual guidance, helping students develop both intellectually and spiritually.

Mr. Hudawi is known for his patience, dedication, and ability to connect with students on a personal level. He believes in creating an inclusive learning environment where every student feels valued and supported in their educational journey.
